MAXHAUST STAGE 4 SOUNDBOOSTER

The Maxhaust sound booster is an electrical module with software from Maxhaust. This module amplifies the engine sound acoustically, integrates additional functions and makes the system compatible with other vehicles. The system is controlled via existing buttons or switches on and around the steering wheel or via the app on your smart phone. For a stage 4 it concerns an ESM (electronic sound module) module where you can choose from 5 different sound profiles. In this module pops and bangs are adjustable.
